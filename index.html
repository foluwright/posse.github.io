Pilots

Flying with Airway Express can be one of the most rewarding experiences of your aviation career. We are committed to compensating for experience and focused on quality of life for our aircrew. As a crewmember with Airway, you will experience flying local routes while enjoying the benefit of being home based.

Flight-attendants

Join the Airway family where we encompass more than 300 professional flight attendants from 6 different US bases. Our flight attendants share a collective passion for caring for our customers. Our crew aims to exceed expectations, inspire through genuine hospitality, and ensure the safety of each and every customer onboard.


Enjoy the diversity of experiences, cultures, and people with each flight.
Do you desire to work for a company that strongly promotes from within? Airway Express has an excellent initial new hire training program. Within six months you may be eligible to join our exemplary purser team. The sky is the limit.

Mechanics

Our maintenance teams provide 24/7 coverage around the nation, dedicated to ensuring a safe flight for passengers and crew. We value the critical skill sets and aircraft knowledge that comes with being a mechanic with Airway. Grow your career from the ground up at any of our bases or at our corporate headquarters in Atlanta, GA.

Our comprehensive benefits package is designed with you in mind. Medical, dental, vision, company paid life insurance, 401K with company match, employee assistance program and more!

Operations & Administrative

The administrative team at Airway Express pride ourselves on providing our employees with work experience built on family values. Your contributions to the team impact the lives of passengers on a daily basis.

Our comprehensive benefits package is designed with you in mind. Medical, dental, vision, company paid life insurance, 401K with company match, employee assistance program and more!
